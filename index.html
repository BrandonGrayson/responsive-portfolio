Anime Vs UFC

After surveying the work force it was discovered that employees liked both Anime and UFC.The CEO of the company asked its development department to build something that encomapassed these 2 concepts. Thus Anime vs UFC was born. Allowing employees to watch both a UFC fight and an anime fight. After the fight is over employees can vote on their favorite. At the end of the week the winner is announced.

Day Planner

After weeks of going over employee performance. It was discovered to often employees end up missing events. After a meeting with management the senior Vice President of workforce asked the development team to build a work day planner for the employees. The planner would need to allow employee's to track their events. Red for times that already passed, Grey for the current time, and green for time that is still left that can be scheduled

Generate Password

Earlier this month different team managers gathered to discuss the rise in employee lateness.After speaking with workforce it was discovered that employee passwords expired every week. The process of finding a password that met the needed criteria can be challenging. The team managers approached the development team and asked them to build an application that allowed employees to generate a random password based on selected criteria.
